Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

At SafeLink, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Learning Progress: Quiz scores, completion status, and learning achievements.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform (pages visited, time spent, etc.).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve our educational services
  • Track your learning progress and personalize your experience
  • Send important updates about your account and new content
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

Currently, SafeLink stores your data locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We do not transmit your information to external servers (in this demo version)
  • You can clear your data anytime by clearing your browser cache

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of SafeLink and our users

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of promotional communications

6. Cookies and Tracking

SafeLink uses browser storage (localStorage) to maintain your login session and save progress. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

SafeLink is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
