Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is SafeLink really free?

Yes! All our core learning modules, quizzes, and safety tips are completely free. We believe digital security education should be accessible to everyone.

Do I need technical knowledge to use SafeLink?

Not at all! Our content is designed for all skill levels. We start with fundamentals and gradually build up to more advanced topics.

How often is content updated?

We update our content regularly to address new threats and attack vectors. Major updates happen monthly, with critical security alerts pushed immediately.

Can I use SafeLink for corporate training?

Absolutely! We offer enterprise solutions with custom content, analytics, and support. Contact us for more information.
